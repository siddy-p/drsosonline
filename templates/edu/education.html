Am I Eligible for MBBS Abroad?

If you meet these basic criteria, you are eligible. Book a free session and we will match you with the best university.

  • Passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Biology (BiPC)
  • NEET qualified (50th percentile for General, 40th for OBC/SC/ST)
  • Age 17+ at the time of admission
  • Valid Indian passport (or willing to apply)
